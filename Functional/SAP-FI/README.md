# SAP FI (Financial Accounting)

## Overview

SAP FI (Financial Accounting) is a core SAP ERP module that helps organizations manage financial transactions, accounting records, and statutory reporting. It enables businesses to maintain accurate financial data and produce financial statements in compliance with accounting standards.

---

## What Does SAP FI Do?

SAP FI is responsible for recording, processing, and reporting all financial transactions within an organization.

It helps companies:

- Maintain General Ledger accounts
- Process Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable
- Manage Asset Accounting
- Perform Bank Accounting
- Generate financial reports
- Support statutory and tax compliance

---

## Real-World Example

A manufacturing company purchases raw materials from a vendor.

1. SAP MM creates the Purchase Order.
2. Goods are received.
3. The vendor invoice is posted.
4. SAP FI records the financial transaction.
5. Payment is processed using the Automatic Payment Program.
6. Financial reports are updated automatically.
