# SAP PM (Plant Maintenance)

> **SAP PM (Plant Maintenance)** is a core SAP Functional module that helps organizations manage the maintenance of equipment, machinery, and technical assets. It enables preventive, predictive, and corrective maintenance to improve asset reliability, reduce downtime, and optimize maintenance costs.

# Overview

SAP PM enables organizations to efficiently plan, execute, monitor, and analyze maintenance activities for physical assets such as machinery, production equipment, vehicles, buildings, and utilities.

The module supports the complete maintenance lifecycle—from equipment registration and maintenance planning to work execution and reporting.

Organizations use SAP PM to:

- Manage equipment and technical assets
- Schedule preventive maintenance
- Handle equipment breakdowns
- Process maintenance work orders
- Track maintenance history
- Improve equipment availability
- Reduce maintenance costs

# Real-World Example

A manufacturing company performs preventive maintenance on production machinery every three months.

1. A Maintenance Plan automatically generates a Maintenance Order.
2. Technicians receive work assignments.
3. Spare parts are issued from inventory through SAP MM.
4. Maintenance work is completed and confirmed.
5. Costs are posted to SAP FI and SAP CO.
6. Equipment history is updated for future analysis.
