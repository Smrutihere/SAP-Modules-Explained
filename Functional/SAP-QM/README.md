# SAP QM (Quality Management)

> **SAP QM (Quality Management)** is a core SAP Functional module that helps organizations plan, inspect, control, and improve product quality throughout the procurement, production, and sales processes. It ensures products meet quality standards while reducing defects and improving customer satisfaction.

# Overview

SAP QM enables organizations to manage quality planning, inspection, notifications, audits, and quality improvement initiatives. It supports quality control across the entire supply chain—from incoming raw materials to finished goods delivered to customers.

Organizations use SAP QM to:

- Inspect incoming materials
- Monitor production quality
- Manage customer complaints
- Record quality defects
- Improve manufacturing processes
- Ensure regulatory compliance

# Real-World Example

A pharmaceutical company receives raw materials from a supplier.

1. SAP MM records the Goods Receipt.
2. SAP QM automatically creates an Inspection Lot.
3. Quality inspectors perform laboratory tests.
4. Results are recorded in SAP.
5. A Usage Decision is made.
6. Approved materials move to inventory.
7. Rejected materials are blocked or returned to the supplier.
