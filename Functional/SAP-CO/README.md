# Overview

SAP CO (Controlling) is designed for internal management reporting and cost control. While SAP FI focuses on external financial reporting, SAP CO helps organizations understand where money is spent, how profitable different business areas are, and how costs can be optimized.

SAP CO enables organizations to:

- Monitor operational costs
- Plan and control budgets
- Analyze profitability
- Allocate overhead costs
- Track product costs
- Support management decision-making

# Key Components

| Component | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Cost Center Accounting (CCA) | Tracks departmental costs |
| Internal Orders | Monitors temporary projects and expenses |
| Profit Center Accounting (PCA) | Measures profitability by business unit |
| Product Cost Controlling (PC) | Calculates manufacturing costs |
| Profitability Analysis (CO-PA) | Analyzes profit by product, customer, or market |
| Cost Element Accounting | Classifies and records costs |

# Real-World Example

A manufacturing company wants to understand the production cost of a new product.

1. SAP PP manages production.
2. SAP MM records raw material consumption.
3. SAP FI records financial transactions.
4. SAP CO collects production costs.
5. Product Cost Controlling calculates the manufacturing cost.
6. CO-PA analyzes product profitability.
7. Management uses reports to improve pricing and reduce costs.
