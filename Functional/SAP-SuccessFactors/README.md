# Overview

SAP SuccessFactors is one of the world's leading cloud Human Capital Management (HCM) solutions. It enables organizations to manage employees throughout their entire employment lifecycle while improving employee engagement, productivity, and compliance.

Organizations use SAP SuccessFactors to:

- Recruit and hire employees
- Onboard new hires
- Manage employee information
- Track goals and performance
- Deliver employee training
- Manage compensation
- Plan workforce succession
- Support payroll integration

# Real-World Example

A multinational company plans to hire 500 employees across different countries.

1. HR creates job requisitions in Recruiting.
2. Candidates apply through the recruitment portal.
3. Selected candidates complete the Onboarding process.
4. Employee data is stored in Employee Central.
5. Managers assign goals and training.
6. Performance reviews are conducted annually.
7. Compensation planning is completed during appraisal cycles.
