# Overview

SAP MM (Materials Management) supports the complete procurement lifecycle, from requesting materials to paying suppliers. It ensures that the right materials are available at the right time while optimizing inventory and purchasing costs.

Organizations use SAP MM to:

- Procure materials and services
- Manage inventory
- Maintain vendor information
- Process purchase orders
- Track goods movements
- Verify supplier invoices
- Control procurement costs

# Real-World Example

A retail company needs to purchase laptops for new employees.

1. The department creates a Purchase Requisition.
2. Purchasing converts it into a Purchase Order.
3. The supplier delivers the laptops.
4. Warehouse receives the goods using MIGO.
5. Quality checks are completed if required.
6. Finance verifies the supplier invoice using MIRO.
7. SAP FI processes payment to the supplier.
