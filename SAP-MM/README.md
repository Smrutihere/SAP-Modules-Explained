# SAP MM (Materials Management)

## 📖 Overview

SAP Materials Management (MM) is a core SAP module that manages procurement, inventory, and material movement within an organization.

It helps businesses purchase goods and services efficiently while maintaining optimal inventory levels.

---

## 🎯 Business Processes

SAP MM supports:

- Procurement
- Purchase Requisitions
- Purchase Orders
- Vendor Management
- Goods Receipt
- Invoice Verification
- Inventory Management

---

## 🔗 Integration

SAP MM commonly integrates with:

- SAP FI (Financial Accounting)
- SAP SD (Sales & Distribution)
- SAP PP (Production Planning)
- SAP EWM (Extended Warehouse Management)
- SAP QM (Quality Management)
